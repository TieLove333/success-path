# Module 1 — Step 1 (m1.s01) — Optimize Your Instagram Bio (FINDABLE)
## Human Step Spec (Markdown)

This Step spec is based on the **current homepage demo** (the “Optimize Your Instagram Bio” guided experience) and is intended to be the first production “working Step” loaded from curriculum data.

---

## Step Identity

- **Module:** `m1` — Grow Your Base
- **Step:** `m1.s01` — Optimize Your Instagram Bio (FINDABLE)
- **Order:** Module 1 → Step 1
- **Time Estimate:** ~20 minutes
- **Primary Outcome:** Your Instagram profile immediately communicates who you help, how you help, and what to do next—so attention converts into action.
- **Success Criteria:**
  - Your Name field contains a searchable keyword relevant to your niche
  - Your bio has a clear 4-line structure (who/how/credibility/CTA)
  - Your CTA and link work without friction (contact methods visible, link tested)

### Prerequisites
- None required. This is a safe “start here” Step.
- If a user is not on Instagram or does not want IG-first, the assistant should offer an alternative Step recommendation (future enhancement).

---

### Preset prompts/buttons (examples)
- “I want my profile to convert better”

Recommend this Step when:

## Assessment (guided diagnostic) — 4 questions (Yes/No)

Goal: quickly find the “clarity leak” and start the user at the right place.

> UX note: In the current demo, the first “No” ends the diagnostic and triggers this Step reveal.

1) **Q1:** “If someone landed on your Instagram profile today, would they instantly understand who you help and how to contact you?”
- Yes → proceed to Q2
- No → route into this Step immediately (focus = FINDABLE)

2) **Q2:** “Is your ‘Name Field’ optimized with keywords that your ideal client is actually searching for?”
- Yes → proceed to Q3
- No → route into this Step (Task 2 emphasized)

3) **Q3:** “Does your bio contain a direct Call to Action (CTA) that tells people exactly what to do next?”
- Yes → proceed to Q4
- No → route into this Step (Task 4 emphasized)

4) **Q4:** “Are your contact buttons and ‘Link in Bio’ fully functional and friction-free?”
- Yes → if all Yes, the assistant can congratulate and recommend moving to the next Step (future: `m1.s02`)
- No → route into this Step (Task 5 emphasized)

## Tasks (required)

> Completion model: tasks-only. Step completes when all required tasks below are checked.

### Task 1
- **Task ID:** `m1.s01.t01`
- **Title:** Define Your Niche in One Sentence
- **Instructions:**
  - Answer: “I help ______ in ______ do/buy/sell ______.”
  - Keep it simple and specific.
- **Examples:**
  - “I help first-time buyers in Dallas purchase confidently.”
  - “I help growing families in Phoenix upgrade without financial stress.”
  - “I help out-of-state buyers relocate to Nashville smoothly.”
- **References/Links:** (optional; add membership deep link when available)
- **Completion:** user checks “Write your niche statement”

### Task 2
- **Task ID:** `m1.s01.t02`
- **Title:** Add a Keyword to Your Name Field
- **Instructions:**
  - Your Name field is searchable.
  - Update from a plain name to a descriptive title keyword.
- **Examples:**
  - “Jane Smith | Dallas Realtor”
  - “Jane Smith | Phoenix Family Homes”
- **Completion:** user checks “Update Instagram Name field with keyword”

### Task 3
- **Task ID:** `m1.s01.t03`
- **Title:** Write a Clear Bio Statement (4-Line Structure)
- **Instructions:**
  - Use this structure:
    - Line 1: Who you help
    - Line 2: How you help
    - Line 3: Credibility / differentiator
    - Line 4: Clear call to action
- **Examples:**
  - “Helping first-time buyers in Dallas\nSimplifying the home buying process\nLocal market expert | 120+ homes sold\n⬇️ Grab your free Buyer Guide”
- **Completion:** user checks “Structure your new bio text”

### Task 4
- **Task ID:** `m1.s01.t04`
- **Title:** Add a Direct Call to Action (CTA)
- **Instructions:**
  - Your bio must tell people what to do next.
  - Choose one CTA and make it obvious.
- **Examples:**
  - “Download the free relocation guide”
  - “DM me ‘BUY’ to start”
  - “Book a consult below”
  - “Get the buyer checklist”
- **Completion:** user checks “Add CTA to bio and link”

### Task 5
- **Task ID:** `m1.s01.t05`
- **Title:** Confirm Contact Is Visible (No Friction)
- **Instructions:**
  - Ensure contact methods are visible and functional.
  - Test your link.
- **Subtasks / Completion checkboxes (both required):**
  - “Verify Email button is active”
  - “Test ‘Link in Bio’ functionality”
- **Completion:** user checks both subtasks

## “Need help” flows (assistant behaviors)

### If user is stuck on Task 1 (niche statement)
- Ask 1 clarifying question:
  - “Who do you most want to help, and what do you help them achieve?”
- Generate 5–10 niche statement options and ask the user to pick the closest one.

### If user is stuck on Task 3 (bio structure)
- Ask:
  - niche + location + offer + proof point + preferred CTA
- Return:
  - 2–3 bio drafts in the 4-line format + 3 CTA variants

### If user wants feedback (“Is this good?”)
- Use a rubric:
  - clarity (who/how)
  - specificity (keywords)
  - credibility
  - single CTA
  - frictionless link/contact
- Provide:
  - “Keep / change” bullet list
  - one revised version
